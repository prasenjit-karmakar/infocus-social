Let's introduce ourselves !

"Infocus" is a social organization formed in 2015, by a group of energetic youths of Mohanpur subdivision from different social and professional backgrond. It is approved by NYK (Nehru Yuva Kendra). Our primary objective is to inculcate the cultural environment, exercise education through different social developmental activities.

MegaQuiz 2019 - General Information

  • Infocus Mega Quiz - 2019 will be held on 8th September,2019, Sunday at Sansktritik Bhawan, Airan Chowmohani, Mohanpur
  • 2(two) persons are required to form each team.
  • There is no age limitation of the participants.
  • Participating team must pay Rs. 40 as Registration Fee at the time of registration.
  • Registration starts from 1st August 2019 and it will be closed on 31st August, 2019
  • A registration token will be issued to each registered team.
  • Participating teams shall have to bring a BLACK BALL POINT PEN and the registration token only inside the exam hall during the screening test.
  • All the teams have to go through a preliminary screening test.
  • Participating teams shall have to report at 9:00am for appearing in the screening test at Sansktritik Bhawan, Airan Chowmohani, Mohanpur.
  • Infocus Mega quiz-2019 will be inaugurated at 11:00 am on the said date, in presence of eminent personalities of Tripura.
  • Main Quiz Competition will be started from 12O’clock.
  • Light refreshment will be provided to each participating team.
  • Use of Mobile phone or any electronic gadgets are not allowed inside the hall room.
  • Infocus will not be liable for the belongings of participants/audience during the entire quiz program.
  • Rules & Regulations of Quiz Contest will be given by the Quiz Master or Organizer.

Blood Bank

"Infocus Blood Bank" is an initiative to create a local database of blood donars with their contact details and blood group information. Health care facilities in rural areas of the state is not up to the standard and many families face difficulty in finding blood of required group during medical emergency. This is our attempt to help those people in times of medical emergency by providing details of donars when requested.
